# Doctrine

## Product thesis

Road Master turns driving theory into a graph-structured narrative combat world that behaves like a memory palace. The product wins if it improves engagement, retention, readiness, and the quality of product data without collapsing into empty gamification.

## Non-goals

- No breadth-first fake Duolingo behavior before the vertical slice is proven.
- No global leaderboard design that punishes beginners.
- No social, B2B, or personalization surface that weakens the foundations contract.
- No hardcoded Chapter I logic inside the engine.

## Voice rules

- Mentor tone: severe, sparse, ceremonial.
- Reward tone: earned, restrained, visible.
- Failure tone: direct, corrective, non-shaming.
- Doctrine style: short lines that can be repeated and remembered.

## Proof criteria

- The system can be described in precise entities and flows instead of vibes.
- A static chapter pack can drive map, combat, pacing, memory, and telemetry without engine changes.
- The MVP can prove the thesis without broad curriculum coverage.
